The Best Movies According to Troy

My top 3 movies of all-Time

Iron Man

This is the movie about my most favorite superhero. It was also the beginning of the Marvel Universe.

Empire Strikes Back

This was the first Star Wars movie I saw when I was kid. I was so caught up in this movie that I cried when Luke's hand was cut off.

Alien

This movie was the first of its genre. I had never been more scared during a movie than this one. Its sequel "Aliens" was equally captivating.
